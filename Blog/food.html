Why This Salad Shines

Coconut-Lime Crema blends coconut milk, lime, and honey for a refreshing finish.

‚ú® Try mint leaves or toasted coconut for extra flavor!

Ingredients by Color

  • Red/Pink: Strawberries, Watermelon, Raspberries
  • Orange/Yellow: Mango, Pineapple, Mandarin Oranges
  • Green: Kiwi, Green Grapes
  • Purple/Blue: Blueberries, Blackberries

Steps to Make

  1. Whisk coconut milk, lime juice, zest, and honey.
  2. Slice and chill fruits for 20+ minutes.
  3. Combine fruits and drizzle crema.
  4. Serve cold and enjoy!

Optional Toppings

  • Toasted coconut flakes
  • Chopped mint or basil
  • Chia seeds or granola
  • Drizzle of honey or agave
